Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

At BXC Staking Platform, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Wallet addresses
  • Transaction history
  • IP address and device information

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the Platform:

  • Log data and analytics
  • Browser type and version
  • Time and date of access
  • Pages visited

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing transactions
  • Improving user experience
  • Communicating with you
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third parties with your consent

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve our services
  • Provide personalized content

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@bxcstaking.com
